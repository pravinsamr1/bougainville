Memorable Experiences by the Bay

Relax by the bay at our Chennai private beach resort, playing around in the waves with your family and friends, or spending some valuable time with your spouse. Create unforgettable memories when you visit us at Bougainville Resorts By The Sea.

Nestled along the pristine shores of Kovalam, our resort offers a sanctuary of tranquility where the gentle breeze and soothing waves create a perfect backdrop for rejuvenation. Whether you are strolling along the golden sands, enjoying beachside activities, or watching the sunset with your loved ones, every moment is crafted to offer you a premium coastal experience.

Pack Your Bags for a Family Adventure

Have a family leisure vacation by the bay with your loved ones. Find the space you need to enjoy quality time together all while saving up to 10% on food & beverage, spa therapies, and laundry services. Keep the kids engaged and active throughout your stay with complimentary access to our kids zone and daily activities.

Our resort is designed to cater to guests of all ages, offering a perfect mix of relaxation for adults and entertainment for children. From sandcastle building on the beach to engaging craft workshops at our dedicated kids' play park, we ensure that your children have a memorable and safe holiday. Create lasting family bonds over beach volleyball or a cozy evening campfire under the stars.

Shine Spa at Bougainville

Our beachside resort in Kovalam makes it easy to relax and unwind during your stay along the scenic ECR. The Shine Spa at Bougainville Resorts By The Sea offers curated wellness packages, rejuvenating body massages, and purifying facial treatments designed to restore your inner balance.

Step into a world of ultimate tranquility at our oceanfront spa facility. Our certified therapists utilize premium organic botanical extracts and ancient wellness traditions to melt away stress. Treat yourself to signature therapies that blend local ingredients with international techniques, leaving you refreshed, revitalized, and ready to enjoy the rest of your seaside getaway.

Sustainable Coastal Travel

The Sustainable Coastal Travel initiative at Bougainville Resorts By The Sea offers meaningful travel experiences, allowing you the opportunity to create a positive environmental and social impact as you explore Kovalam and build deeper connections with the local community.

We are committed to preserving the natural beauty of the Chennai coastline and supporting the local community. Through our program, guests can participate in beach cleaning drives, learn about turtle conservation efforts along the Bay of Bengal, and support local artisanal workshops. Travel with purpose and leave a positive legacy behind while enjoying a luxury beachfront stay.
