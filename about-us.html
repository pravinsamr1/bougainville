Luxury Beach Resort in Kovalam

Bougainville Resorts By The Sea

Bougainville Resorts By The Sea, formerly Casuarina Bay, brings together a relaxed coastal setting, premium stays, curated dining, and event-ready spaces beside Kovalam beach near ECR Chennai.

Designed for weekend escapes, family holidays, corporate outings, celebrations, and quiet seaside breaks, the resort offers private air-conditioned yurts, deluxe rooms, pool access, sea facing restaurant experiences, open air BBQ nights, live music evenings, and attentive guest service. Whether you are swimming in our pool, walking on the beach, or relaxing in the gardens, every moment is crafted to help you unwind.

Distinctive Stays

Private Mongolian style air-conditioned yurts and comfortable deluxe rooms create a memorable poolside stay close to the sea, offering the perfect combination of modern amenities and natural coastal beauty.

Dining & Events

From sunset meals and romantic candlelit dinners to open-air BBQ nights and corporate day-outs, every experience is carefully shaped around our beautiful beachside setting to create lasting memories.

Why Guests Choose Us

Beachside Experience

Pool Facing Luxury Yurts

Stay in private air-conditioned yurts with a unique resort feel, modern comfort, and easy access to poolside relaxation. Each yurt features premium bedding, warm lighting, and private sit-out spaces perfect for starlit nights.

Sea Facing Restaurant

Enjoy multicuisine dining, fresh seafood grills, open-air BBQ nights, live acoustic music, and romantic candlelit dinners with coastal views of the Bay of Bengal. Our chefs customize dishes to match your palate.

Celebrations & Outings

Plan seamless corporate day outs, professional conferences, birthdays, wedding anniversaries, family packages, and team-building experiences by the sea. We provide customized setups, audio-visual gear, and catering.

Comfort & Convenience

Amenities For A Relaxed Stay

24 Hours Security

Round-the-clock security and CCTV monitoring help guests and their families enjoy their beachside stay with complete confidence, privacy, and peace of mind.

Room Service

Convenient and prompt service support is available throughout the day for families, couples, and group guests, bringing delicious hot food and refreshments directly to your door.

Wifi & Parking

High-speed fiber-optic Wi-Fi and spacious, secure car parking make both leisure stays, work-from-anywhere setups, and large events or gatherings effortless.

About Us

Bougainville Resorts By The Sea is a luxury beachside resort in Kovalam offering private yurts, premium dining, swimming pools, beach events, and unforgettable holiday experiences near Chennai.
