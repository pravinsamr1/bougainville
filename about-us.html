Luxury Beach Resort in Kovalam

Bougainville Resorts By The Sea

Bougainville Resorts By The Sea, formerly Casuarina Bay, brings together a relaxed coastal setting, premium stays, curated dining, and event-ready spaces beside Kovalam beach near ECR Chennai.

Designed for weekend escapes, family holidays, corporate outings, celebrations, and quiet seaside breaks, the resort offers private air-conditioned yurts, deluxe rooms, pool access, sea facing restaurant experiences, open air BBQ nights, live music evenings, and attentive guest service.

Distinctive Stays

Private Mongolian style air-conditioned yurts and comfortable rooms create a memorable poolside stay close to the sea.

Dining & Events

From sunset meals and candle light dinners to BBQ nights and corporate gatherings, every experience is shaped around the beachside setting.

Why Guests Choose Us
