Bougainville Resorts By The Sea

Discover a breathtaking oasis where the gentle whispers of the Bay of Bengal meet unmatched luxury. Formerly known as Casuarina Bay, Bougainville Resorts By The Sea invites you to immerse yourself in tropical tranquility. Unwind in private, air-conditioned Mongolian yurts, indulge in sea-facing culinary masterworks, and celebrate life's milestones under a canopy of stars—all along the picturesque ECR near Chennai.
